Delamater: Sage Partner Summit 2021 Twitter Sentiment Analysis

What is Sage Summit? Before the pandemic, it was truly a sight to behold. I've presented at more than 10 or so of these and it's a real blast to get to know cusotmers and partners. In this analysis I take a look at the tweets that tagged SagePartnerSummit and analyze the sentiment. Usually, when keynote presentations are made partners are pretty positive on their tweets, but we will find out if our NLP processor can detect outliers and or find a trend.

Note: Custom twitter extraction code was removed from the github check-in as it contains private twitter developer keys.
